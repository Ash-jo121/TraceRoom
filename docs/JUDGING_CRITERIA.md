# Judging Criteria Mapping

## Potential Impact

TraceRoom addresses auditability, governance, and incident reconstruction for autonomous financial-agent decisions. The agent workflow is the observed workload; TraceRoom and SigNoz form the product's audit layer.

## Creativity And Innovation

The product combines evidence validation, deterministic governance, replay, human-readable decision reconstruction, and SigNoz-backed forensic workflows.

## Technical Excellence

The demo uses a repeatable market snapshot, validated structured agent outputs, deterministic evidence and risk checks, SQLite persistence, and OpenTelemetry instrumentation.

## Best Use Of SigNoz

TraceRoom uses SigNoz as a core product surface: end-to-end agent traces, correlated logs, LLM and decision metrics, Decision Integrity and Agent Reliability dashboards, alerts, and MCP-backed investigation.

## User Experience

The Command Center explains a decision in domain language and deep-links into supporting SigNoz traces, logs, and dashboards without rebuilding Trace Explorer.

## Presentation Quality

The demo moves between TraceRoom's readable audit view and SigNoz's technical evidence, showing healthy, evidence-fault, risk-veto, error, and deadlock replay sessions.
